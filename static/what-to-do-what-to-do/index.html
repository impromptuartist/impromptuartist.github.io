What to do? What to do.

October 18, 2016 // Tagged in: Life, Web Design

I make things difficult for myself.

I'm a perfectionist. It's what I do.

This new venture is no different. What should I use for a blog theme? Should I get a pre-made theme? Should I make my own?

Make my own, of course! - Mark Stringer 2016

Ah yes, the famous can-do attitude. Here comes the problem. If it's not perfect at first, it's not ever going to be good enough. We all know that's bullshit and yet here I am, stuck in the same cycle.

Enough.

It's not something I should do, it's something I can do.

Mom's are good for many things, not the least of which is motivation. Something she told me rings very true in making this blog. Telling myself I should do something is very different than telling myself I can do something.

Should creates guilt. Can creates options.

So what does that mean?

It means that I'm going to keep this default theme for a little bit while I make my own... and that's okay. Let's try something new for a change. Good enough will work for now and better will come soon. It always does.

Until next time.
